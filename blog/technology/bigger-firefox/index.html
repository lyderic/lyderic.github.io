Make your Firefox bigger

8 décembre 2024

For me, Firefox in its default layout is barely legible, because everything is too small. I could use the zoom function, but it only zooms the content of a page, not the rest (tabs’ text, search bar, icons…)

To make the whole of Firefox bigger, look for layout.css.dev in about:config and change the value to something bigger than 1. I chose 1.25.
